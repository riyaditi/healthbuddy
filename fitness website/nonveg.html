Healthy Non-Vegetarian Recipes

Grilled Chicken

A healthy grilled chicken recipe with a mix of herbs and olive oil.

How to make: Preheat the grill or grill pan. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Grill for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy!

Salmon Salad

A protein-rich salmon salad with fresh greens and dressing.

How to make: Mix 1 can (or 1 cooked fillet) of flaked salmon with 2 tbsp mayo or Greek yogurt, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, and 1 tbsp lemon juice. Add 1 finely chopped celery stalk, 2 tbsp chopped red onion, and 1 tbsp fresh dill or parsley (optional). Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything well to combine. Serve on lettuce, toast, or in a wrap. Enjoy your quick and tasty salmon salad!

Egg Scramble

A quick and nutritious scrambled egg dish with vegetables.

How to make: Whisk 2 eggs with salt and pepper.Heat a pan with butter on low heat.Pour eggs in, stir gently with a spatula.Cook until soft and slightly runny.Remove from heat and serve immediately. Enjoy!

Turkey Wrap

A light and filling turkey wrap with fresh veggies.

How to make: Spread mayonnaise or hummus on a large tortilla. Layer turkey slices, chopped lettuce, sliced tomatoes, cucumber, and cheese (optional). Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the sides. Slice in half and serve immediately. Quick, healthy, and delicious!

Tuna Sandwich

A delicious tuna sandwich packed with protein.

How to make: Mix drained tuna with mayonnaise and black pepper. Spread the mixture on one slice of bread. Add lettuce and tomato slices on top. Cover with the second slice of bread, cut in half, and serve. Simple, tasty, and ready in minutes! Enjoy!
