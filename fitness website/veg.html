Healthy Vegetarian Recipes

1. Quinoa Salad

A refreshing salad with quinoa, cucumbers, tomatoes, and a lemon dressing.

How to make:Cook 1 cup quinoa with 2 cups water or broth for about 15 minutes. Let it cool. Chop 1 cucumber, 1/4 cup red onion, and 1 cup cherry tomatoes. In a small bowl, mix 3 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt, and pepper for the dressing. Combine the quinoa with veggies, 1/4 cup chopped parsley, feta, and olives. Toss with the dressing and serve chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy!

2. Paneer Stir Fry

A quick and tasty stir fry with paneer, bell peppers, and Indian spices.

How to make:Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and sauté sliced onion and bell pepper until soft. Add 200g paneer cubes and stir-fry until golden. Sprinkle 1/2 tsp each of turmeric, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well to coat everything and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with roti or rice. Enjoy!

3. Spinach & Chickpea Curry

A protein-rich curry made with spinach and chickpeas in a flavorful sauce.

How to make: Heat 1 tbsp oil, sauté chopped onion until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute. Add chopped tomato, turmeric, cumin powder, and salt, cooking until the tomato softens. Stir in chickpeas and spinach, cook until the spinach wilts. Add 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional) and simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or roti. Enjoy!

4. Vegetable Stir-Fried Noodles

A delicious mix of noodles, fresh vegetables, and soy-based sauce.

How to make: Cook noodles according to package instructions and set aside. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, sauté minced garlic, then add julienned carrots, bell pepper, and shredded cabbage. Add cooked noodles, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional), and pepper. Toss well and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot. Enjoy!

5. Stuffed Bell Peppers

Bell peppers filled with quinoa, beans, and a tasty tomato sauce.

How to make: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Sauté chopped onions and tomatoes with cumin powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add cooked rice and chickpeas, mix well. Stuff the mixture into bell peppers and place them in a baking dish. Top with cheese (optional) and bake for 20-25 minutes. Serve hot and enjoy!
